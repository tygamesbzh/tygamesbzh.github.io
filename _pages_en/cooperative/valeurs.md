values:
  - id: respect
    number: "01"
    heading: "Respect for creative works"
    intro: "Every game we sign is treated with the same care, regardless of its status or financial success."
    bullets:
      - title: "Equal treatment"
        body: "All signed games receive the same attention, regardless of their visibility or sales."
      - title: "Ownership stays with the creators"
        body: "Works always remain the property of their developers."
      - title: "Fair terms"
        body: "Publishing and distribution terms are fair and the same for everyone. The contract is non-binding."
      - title: "Profits reinvested"
        body: "Profits are reinvested as much as possible across all projects, by collective decision."
  - id: transparence
    number: "02"
    heading: "Transparency"
    intro: "We account for our decisions and provide access to data relevant to members."
    bullets:
      - title: "Access to sales figures"
        body: "Studios and creators have unconditional access to sales figures across all platforms for their productions, within the timeframes imposed by those platforms."
      - title: "Regular reporting"
        body: "We commit to answering all questions about the company's operations or transactions, and we report on our actions regularly."
      - title: "Members consulted"
        body: "Engaged members are consulted on decisions and strategies."
  - id: inclusivite
    number: "03"
    heading: "Inclusivity & fighting discrimination"
    intro: "We want to put our services at the disposal of the creators who need them most."
    bullets:
      - title: "Open to everyone"
        body: "Enabling minorities and people who face discrimination to access publishing and distribution services for their games."
      - title: "Committed works"
        body: "We support and champion works with a message, a cause, or lived experience behind them."
  - id: no-ia
    number: "04"
    heading: "No to generative AI"
    intro: "Our position on generative AI is simple and firm. We believe it undermines artists, sanitises creativity, and consumes unreasonable resources."
    bullets:
      - title: "Visuals, audio, text, translations = hard no"
        body: "No game containing AI-generated visual or audio elements will be accepted or retained in the catalogue."
      - title: "Source code: open to discussion"
        body: "Using AI tools for development is tolerated, as they are ubiquitous in the profession and in IDEs. <strong>BUT</strong> the developer must have full technical mastery of the code and the project. And TyGAMES funding must not be used to pay for subscriptions or tokens."
