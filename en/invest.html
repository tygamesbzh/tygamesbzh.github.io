Important reminders

Investing in a cooperative is not a traditional financial investment.

Only invest what you can afford to lose

Like any investment, your capital is exposed to the risk of partial or total loss.

Locked capital

The money invested will be locked for the duration of your membership and is not available in the short term.

No profit on shares

A cooperative does not generate profits on social shares. You will recover the same amount invested when leaving the membership.
