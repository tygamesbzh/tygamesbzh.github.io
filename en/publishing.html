Publisher

We modestly fund services, original creations, and ports. Read our offer, then submit your project.

Applications are reviewed by the Cooperative Council and management, once per quarter.

Budget per game

< €15,000

Our budgets are still very modest and can't cover full production.

With this budget, depending on your needs:

  • ✓Localization (translation) based on word count
  • ✓QA: required on every release
  • ✓Key art
  • ✓Trailer
  • ✓Advertising (Reddit, Meta)
  • ✓Promotional events (GMIF, Pégases, The Mix…)

In return

  • →Your project is followed by a member-mentor
  • →Expenses are tracked on a debt slate
  • →This debt is repaid first (80% TyGAMES, 20% dev)
  • →Once the debt is cleared, the revshare flips in favor of the dev (30% TyGAMES, 70% dev)
  • →The contract cannot be terminated before repayment

Console ports

We do ports in-house, with strong technical expertise and prior experience on each console.

We own devkits and testkits. You can retrieve the source code at the end of the port.
