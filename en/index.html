Who we are

A different cooperative

TyGAMES is an independent video game publisher created in Brittany. We operate as a cooperative: every member is a co-decision-maker, every project is shared.

Our mission: support Breton and independent developers by giving them structure, resources, and visibility without sacrificing their creative freedom.
