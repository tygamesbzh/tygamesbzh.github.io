A different model,
a shared ambition

TyGAMES is a Société Coopérative d'Intérêt Collectif (SCIC) a legal structure that puts creators, members, and the community at the heart of every decision.

04

Revenue sharing

Democratic governance

As a SCIC, TyGAMES applies the principle of one member, one vote. Regardless of the number of shares held, every member participates equally in decisions at the General Assembly.

This structure ensures that the cooperative's direction stays true to its founding values, protected from dominant financial interests.
